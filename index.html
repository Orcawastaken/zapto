What Is ZaptoAnarchy?

An anarchy Minecraft server. One world, no plugins changing how you play. Free Speech is encouraged here; who you support and don't is your opinion, that you can express.

One World, forever

One world, no resets, ever.

Full anarchy

There are zero rules dictating what you do and do not. But the server must follow the laws of the jurisdiction of California in order to remain active.
